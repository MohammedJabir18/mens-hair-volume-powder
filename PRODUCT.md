# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Men with fine, flat, or thinning hair looking for a reliable, fast, and high-performance styling solution. They browse on mobile (often on the go or in ambient lighting) and need quick, scannable visual proof of efficacy.

## Product Purpose
Single-page Direct-to-Consumer (DTC) conversion funnel storefront for VALO Root-Lock Volume Powder. The page must build immediate trust, demonstrate product value, and guide the user seamlessly to purchase.

## Brand Personality
* **Prestige Minimalist:** Sleek, premium, and clean (Alabaster cream and dark stone).
* **Confident & Scientific:** Clean, structured grids and precise descriptions rather than generic marketing buzzwords.
* **Warm & Editorial:** Feels like a high-end personal care journal or magazine.

## Anti-references
* **CCTV Hacker / Monospace HUDs:** Monospace text, bracketed telemetryhud elements, and tech overlays.
* **Generic SaaS Clichés:** High-saturation blue/purple buttons, cards-within-cards, or glassmorphism backgrounds.
* **Low-Contrast Gray Typography:** Thin gray text on off-white backgrounds that compromises readability.

## Design Principles
1. **Accessibility First:** Ensure text is readable with high contrast across all lighting conditions.
2. **Visual Proof Over Claims:** Prioritize the before/after slider and UGC video loops to show real product performance.
3. **Structured Grid Layouts:** Use clean, balanced spacing and bordered grids rather than soft drop-shadowed cards.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
* WCAG AA compliance (contrast ratio ≥4.5:1 for body text, ≥3:1 for large elements).
* Dynamic prefers-color-scheme support (Light/Dark themes).
* Respect reduced motion preferences.
